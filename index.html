About Serendipity

Experience the Ocean
Like Never Before

Welcome aboard Serendipity, an extraordinary charter experience. Based in Saint Petersburg, Florida, Serendipity is an expertly remodeled, stunning 94' Lazzara Hardtop motor yacht.

Located between Tampa and Sarasota on Florida's Gulf Coast, Serendipity offers rare access to the Intercoastal Waterway, opening up hidden islands, exclusive inlets, lively sandbars, and calm anchorages that few yachts of this size can reach.

Whether you're celebrating a milestone or simply seeking peace on the water, Serendipity brings refined luxury, privacy, and adventure together in one unforgettable experience.
